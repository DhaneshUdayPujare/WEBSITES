Our Story

A Place That Feels

Pranaam – A Veg Treat is a beloved vegetarian restaurant in Pimpri-Chinchwad, known for its delicious food, welcoming atmosphere, and exceptional service. Whether it's a family dinner, a quick breakfast, or a celebration, we serve every meal with care and consistency.

Every dish is crafted from the freshest ingredients, inspired by recipes that bring comfort and joy. From our sizzling South Indian breakfast to hearty North Indian curries — this is more than a meal, it's a memory.
